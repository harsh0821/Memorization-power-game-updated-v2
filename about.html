About us

A fun game for your ambitious children and toddlers to check their memorization skills This game combines the visual memorization power and sensory listening power of the user and analyze the result to tell you how strong is your memorization power.

This is a fun game that I made for my project. It basically analyses your game result and tells you how you performed as compared to other people. I made a data set of the users results and made it update itself as more and more people use it. As of now, more than 150 people have played and tested this game and helped us train our data model, which increases the accuracy of the results that it provides and can eventually help users increase their memorization power.
